Filipino:

Info:

The history of Filipino cuisine took inspiration from other countries. The Spanish colonization of the Philippines made a mark in Filipino culinary traditions with a majority of Philippine dishes having Spanish origin. Spanish ingredients like tomatoes, potatoes, onions, and garlic were introduced, as well as cooking techniques such as frying and saut√©ing. Filipinos until today inherit this practices of Filipino dishes and skills to their younger generations.

Chinese:

Info:

A common chinese food that's shown in the photo are dumplings. Chinese people have a traditional belief that eating good food can bring harmony and closeness to the family connections. Shopping daily for fresh food is essential for all Chinese cooking. The Chinese select fresh meats and seasonal fruits and vegetables from the local market to ensure freshness.

Thailand :

Info:

The photo is the national food of Thailand Pad Thai, served as street food and the main dish in restaurants. Pad Thai is a stir-fried rice noodle dish prepared with chicken, shrimp, or tofu.
